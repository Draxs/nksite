Hello and thank you for visiting my website. I can’t do my job without people like you so I want you to know how much I appreciate your time and your feedback.

Part of my work is telling a story with a picture: you may notice that I love clothes and I love art and architecture. I studied at art school but finishing college doesn’t mean stopping your education. Every day I learn more though travelling, reading, talking and listening to people. People like you. I aim to make each one of my stories and photos better every time, so please let me know if I’m doing a good job!

I’ve had a varied career and moved countries twice so far in my life. Be brave and say yes is my mantra. I love fashion. But, for me fashion is more than pretty clothes or cool jewellery; it’s the individual statement of your style, your personality. It’s about feeling good about yourself.

I hope my photos and stories can be part of your way to find your individual style. I am still learning and changing, experimenting and having fun. I don’t work with companies who are not ethical, not fair to their workforce or who don’t respect the environment. I don’t wear clothes that don’t suit me or I don’t like, regardless of how many ‘inducements’ I am offered. I think integrity is one of the most important things in your life and you should not comprise.

These are the things that are important to me. Let me know what is important to you.

Nena xx
